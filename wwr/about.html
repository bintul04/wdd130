Our Mission

Our mission is to provide thrilling and safe white water rafting experiences, connecting adventurers with nature while fostering a sense of excitement and community.

History

White water rafting has a rich history that dates back thousands of years. It originated as a means of transport for goods and people along rivers, particularly in regions with rugged terrain. The sport gained popularity in the mid-20th century, with adventurers seeking thrills in the challenging rapids of rivers like the Colorado and the Snake.

In 1972, the first organized rafting trip took place on the Colorado River, marking the beginning of commercial rafting. Since then, the sport has grown exponentially, attracting adventure seekers from all around the globe. Today, we continue to honor this legacy by providing thrilling and safe rafting experiences that connect adventurers with the beauty of nature.
